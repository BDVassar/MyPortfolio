About me

My name is Bryan, welcome to my Portfolio. I am a Full Stack Web Developer that graduated from Boise CodeWorks in February 2023. I am excited to see where this journey takes me! I decided to start my pursuit into engineering with Mechanical Engineering, but fell in love with coding while doing my required 2 semesters of Java. I found Boise CodeWorks and have enjoyed every minute of my struggles to find solutions to problems.
